Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science at Peking University, co-advised by Prof. Xuanzhe Liu and Prof. Xin Jin. I received my B.S. degree (Summa Cum Laude) in artificial intelligence from Turing Class, Peking University, in 2022. My research interests include distributed systems, resource disaggregation, and their intersection with serverless computing.
